# Logo and Name Use Policy

This policy applies to the Technology Club of Thrace name, logo and visual identity as used in this
project.

## Permitted Use

You may use the Technology Club of Thrace logo only to identify the environmental station as the
source of the measurements displayed by this project.

The logo may appear in:

- the main measurements display;
- TV mode or kiosk mode;
- printed or digital documentation describing the measurement source;
- attribution sections required by LICENSE.md and DATA_TERMS.md.

## Requirements

When using the logo, you must:

- keep it visible when station measurements are displayed publicly;
- keep it unmodified, except for proportional resizing;
- avoid distortion, recoloring, cropping or visual effects that change its identity;
- keep the measurement source link or reference close enough to be understood by viewers.

## Not an Endorsement

Use of the logo does not imply that the Technology Club of Thrace endorses a third-party
organization, product, service, event, sponsor or commercial activity.

If you deploy a modified version, you must not suggest that it is an official deployment unless you
have written permission.

## Prohibited Use

You may not use the logo:

- as your own brand;
- to promote unrelated products or services;
- in advertising that is not directly related to the environmental station display;
- in a misleading, disrespectful or confusing way;
- in a way that suggests ownership of the station measurements.

## Removal

The logo and source attribution must not be removed from public displays that use measurements from
the Technology Club of Thrace Environmental Station.
